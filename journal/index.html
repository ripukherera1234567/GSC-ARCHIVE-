The File
They assigned me to review a biological anomaly discovered decades ago. The file was incomplete. Not like pages were missing — it felt more like the file had been written around something. Like someone had removed the middle and stitched the edges back together.

I flagged it to my supervisor, Dr. ████████. He said that was normal. He didn't look at me when he said it.
ENTRY // 03 2026-01-22
I Heard Something
I stayed late tonight. Most of the floor was empty. The lights in the east corridor were off — they're never off.

I heard two people arguing behind a door marked ███-C. I couldn't make out words. Just tone. One of them was scared. The other one was not.

I went home and couldn't sleep. I keep thinking about that file.
