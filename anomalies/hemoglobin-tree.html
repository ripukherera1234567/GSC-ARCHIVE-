File HBT-1054

Status: Contained

Description

An anomalous tree exhibiting organic tissue-like bark and crimson foliage. Growth rate recorded at 1054x standard flora in similar conditions.

Discovery

First documented during the Vietnam conflict by military personnel. Reports describe accelerated environmental influence within a 30-meter radius.

Containment

Specimen relocated to secure bio-site. Surrounding soil quarantined.
